⚡ 3-Step Memory Lock

Simple sequence to push short-term learning into durable memory.

Steps

  1. Revise (2–5 min): Fast skim of the topic; highlight only essentials.
  2. Recall (5 min): Close material → write or speak what you remember.
  3. Retest (later): After ~6 hours or next day, answer 3 quick questions on the same topic.

Why it works

Spacing + active recall = repeat exposure in different contexts. That’s how brain consolidates.
